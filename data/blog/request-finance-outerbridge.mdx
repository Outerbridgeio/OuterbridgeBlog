## Request Finance X Outerbridge

[Request Finance](https://www.request.finance/) is a web application for Companies, DAOs, and Freelancers to easily manage crypto invoices, salaries, and expenses in a fast, non-custodial, and compliant way. Payments can be denominated in local currencies and processed in crypto.

Outerbridge has worked with Request Finance team to have APIs integrated, allowing users to build workflow automation such as getting notification from new invoice, monitor invoice status update, invoice and salary payment creation.
